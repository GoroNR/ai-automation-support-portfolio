# Operating Systems for IT Support

## Project goal

The goal of this study artifact is to understand the Windows and Linux operating-system skills most useful in entry-level IT Support and SaaS Technical Support.

The material was selected from Course 3 of the Google IT Support Professional Certificate:

**Operating Systems and You: Becoming a Power User**

Instead of completing the entire course in one day, I focused on:

- Windows and Linux filesystems,
- command-line basics,
- users, groups and permissions,
- installing and removing software,
- system logs,
- remote connection tools.
