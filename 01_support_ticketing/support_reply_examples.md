# Support Reply Examples

## Cel artefaktu

Celem tego ćwiczenia było porównanie ręcznej odpowiedzi supportowej z wersją poprawioną przez AI według zasad:

* concise,
* empathetic,
* professional,
* no overpromising.

Finalne odpowiedzi są gotowe do dodania do arkusza `Support_Tickets_Demo` w kolumnie `answer_draft`.

---

# Example 1 — Login issue

## Ticket

**Ticket ID:** T009
**Category:** login
**Priority:** high
**Summary:** User cannot log in after changing password.

**Customer message:**
User cannot log in after changing password.

## Draft v1 — manual reply

Hi Emma,

I’m sorry you cannot log in. I will check if your account is locked and if the password reset worked correctly. Please confirm that you are using the correct email address.

Best,
Adrian

## AI improved version

Hi Emma,

Thanks for reaching out. I’m sorry you’re having trouble logging in after changing your password.

I’ll check whether your account is locked and whether the password reset was completed correctly. Please also confirm that you are using the email address connected to your account.

Best,
Adrian

## Final reply

Hi Emma,

Thanks for reaching out. I’m sorry you’re having trouble logging in after changing your password.

I’ll check whether your account is locked and whether the password reset was completed correctly. Please also confirm that you are using the email address connected to your account.

Best,
Adrian

## Comparison

The manual version was correct but very short. The AI improved version sounds more empathetic and professional, while still avoiding promises such as immediate access restoration.

# Example 2 — Billing issue

## Ticket

**Ticket ID:** T010
**Category:** billing
**Priority:** medium
**Summary:** User was charged twice for the same subscription period.

**Customer message:**
User was charged twice for the same subscription period.

## Draft v1 — manual reply

Hi Michael,

Sorry about the double charge. I will check your billing history and see if the second payment was a mistake. If it was, we can help with the next step.

Best,
Adrian

## AI improved version

Hi Michael,

Thanks for contacting support. I’m sorry about the duplicate charge.

I’ll review your billing history and payment records to confirm what happened. If the second charge was processed by mistake, we’ll help you with the appropriate next step according to our billing policy.

Best,
Adrian

## Final reply

Hi Michael,

Thanks for contacting support. I’m sorry about the duplicate charge.

I’ll review your billing history and payment records to confirm what happened. If the second charge was processed by mistake, we’ll help you with the appropriate next step according to our billing policy.

Best,
Adrian

## Comparison

The manual version was clear, but the AI improved version is safer because it does not immediately promise a refund. It explains that the billing history must be checked first.

# Example 3 — Bug report

## Ticket

**Ticket ID:** T011
**Category:** bug
**Priority:** medium
**Summary:** Export to PDF button does not generate a file.

**Customer message:**
Export to PDF button does not generate a file.

## Draft v1 — manual reply

Hi Sophia,

Thanks for reporting this. I will check the PDF export issue. Please tell me what browser you use and if this happens with all reports or only one report.

Best,
Adrian

## AI improved version

Hi Sophia,

Thanks for reporting this. I’m sorry the PDF export is not working.

Could you please confirm which browser you are using and whether the issue happens with all reports or only one specific report? If possible, please also send a screenshot or short recording of what happens after clicking Export to PDF.

Best,
Adrian

## Final reply

Hi Sophia,

Thanks for reporting this. I’m sorry the PDF export is not working.

Could you please confirm which browser you are using and whether the issue happens with all reports or only one specific report? If possible, please also send a screenshot or short recording of what happens after clicking Export to PDF.

Best,
Adrian

## Comparison

The manual version collected the basic information, but the AI improved version is more complete because it asks for browser details, scope of the issue and visual evidence.

# Example 4 — Integration issue

## Ticket

**Ticket ID:** T013
**Category:** integration
**Priority:** high
**Summary:** New leads from website form are not appearing in CRM.

**Customer message:**
New leads from website form are not appearing in CRM.

## Draft v1 — manual reply

Hi Olivia,

Thanks for reporting this. I will check the website form and CRM integration. Please confirm when the last lead was sent correctly and if anything was changed recently.

Best,
Adrian

## AI improved version

Hi Olivia,

Thanks for reporting this. I’ll help investigate the integration issue between your website form and CRM.

Could you please confirm when the last lead was successfully sent and whether anyone changed the form, API key, CRM settings, or automation workflow recently? I’ll also check the integration logs for errors.

Best,
Adrian

## Final reply

Hi Olivia,

Thanks for reporting this. I’ll help investigate the integration issue between your website form and CRM.

Could you please confirm when the last lead was successfully sent and whether anyone changed the form, API key, CRM settings, or automation workflow recently? I’ll also check the integration logs for errors.

Best,
Adrian

## Comparison

The manual version was a good start, but the AI improved version is more useful for SaaS Support because it mentions likely causes: API key, CRM settings, automation workflow and integration logs.

# Example 5 — Performance issue

## Ticket

**Ticket ID:** T014
**Category:** performance
**Priority:** medium
**Summary:** Dashboard loads very slowly after login.

**Customer message:**
Dashboard loads very slowly after login.

## Draft v1 — manual reply

Hi James,

Sorry the dashboard is slow. Please tell me what browser you use and if other people in your team have the same problem. I will check if there are any issues on our side.

Best,
Adrian

## AI improved version

Hi James,

Thanks for reaching out. I’m sorry the dashboard is loading slowly after login.

Could you please confirm which browser you are using, whether the issue also happens in incognito mode, and if other users in your team are experiencing the same problem? I’ll also check whether there are any known performance issues on our side.

Best,
Adrian

## Final reply

Hi James,

Thanks for reaching out. I’m sorry the dashboard is loading slowly after login.

Could you please confirm which browser you are using, whether the issue also happens in incognito mode, and if other users in your team are experiencing the same problem? I’ll also check whether there are any known performance issues on our side.

Best,
Adrian

## Comparison

The manual version asked the right questions, but the AI improved version is more polished and specific. It adds incognito mode as a simple troubleshooting step and keeps the tone professional.

# Summary

In these examples, AI helped improve support replies by making them clearer, more empathetic and more structured.

The biggest improvements were:

* better opening tone,
* more specific troubleshooting questions,
* safer wording without overpromising,
* clearer next steps,
* more professional structure.

This exercise shows how AI can support a SaaS Support Specialist by improving first-draft replies while keeping the final decision and responsibility with the support agent.
